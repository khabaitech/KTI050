Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami.
A boy swallows a cursed talisman - the finger of a demon - and becomes cursed himself.
He enters a shaman's school to be able to locate the demon's other body parts and thus exorcise himself.
