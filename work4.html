Litti Chokha ₹120
Litti Chokha is a Bihari delicacy of Litti, which is a stuffed and baked whole wheat dough balls served with a veggie mash called Chokha.The chokha for litti is made with mashed brinjals, tomatoes, garlic, mustard oil, salt, lemon juice etc.
